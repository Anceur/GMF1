Les Grands Moulins de Frenda

A HISTORICAL GRAIN MILLING LEGACY

1910

In 1910, the first large-scale mill under the name *Les Grands Moulins de Frenda* was founded, solidifying Frenda as a key hub for wheat milling in the region. The mill played a significant role in transforming wheat into flour for local consumption and trade.

1918

After World War I, the region saw a revitalization of farming practices and an increased output of wheat. The milling industry saw significant expansion, with GMF adopting modern milling equipment to meet the demands of the population.

1920

The 1920s brought significant advances in milling technology, with *Les Grands Moulins de Frenda* introducing electric-powered mills, enhancing productivity. The mill became central to local economies and was pivotal in grain processing for the surrounding regions.

1970

The 1970s brought continued growth for GMF, as new investments were made in infrastructure, creating an efficient processing facility that served the expanding needs of the market. The factory helped meet the growing demand for wheat-based products in Algeria and beyond.

2020

Today, *Les Grands Moulins de Frenda* continues to be one of the leading milling companies in the region, with a commitment to sustainable farming practices and modern technological advancements in grain processing.
